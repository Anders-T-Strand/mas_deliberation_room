# Current Marketing Strategy - Local Services

## Business Overview
- **Industry**: Local Home Services (Plumbing, HVAC, Electrical)
- **Service Area**: 25-mile radius, suburban market
- **Average Ticket**: $285
- **Customer Base**: 80% residential, 20% commercial
- **Daily Service Calls**: 45-50

## Current Marketing Mix

### 1. Google Ads (70% of budget)
- **Daily Spend**: $1,200-1,500
- **Focus Keywords**: "emergency plumber near me", "AC repair", "electrician"
- **Average CPC**: $10.00
- **Conversion Rate**: 12%
- **Call-Only Campaigns**: 60% of spend

### 2. SEO & Google My Business (15% of budget)
- **GMB Reviews**: 4.7 stars (850 reviews)
- **Local Pack Rankings**: Top 3 for main keywords
- **Website Traffic**: 8,000 monthly visitors
- **Organic Conversions**: 25% of leads

### 3. Traditional Marketing (10% of budget)
- **Direct Mail**: Quarterly to 10,000 homes
- **Vehicle Wraps**: 15 service vehicles
- **Local Sponsorships**: Youth sports teams

### 4. Customer Retention (5% of budget)
- **Service Agreements**: 450 active
- **Email Reminders**: Seasonal maintenance
- **Loyalty Discounts**: Repeat customer program

## Current Challenges
1. **Seasonal Fluctuations**: 40% revenue drop in spring/fall
2. **Competition**: 15+ competitors in service area
3. **Tech Adoption**: Limited online booking usage
4. **Emergency vs Scheduled**: 25/75 split (want more scheduled)

## Key Metrics
- **Monthly Marketing Spend**: $38,000
- **Cost per Acquisition**: $80
- **Customer Retention Rate**: 73%
- **Repeat Customer Rate**: 40%
- **Average Response Time**: 2.4 hours

## Lead Sources
- **Google Ads**: 45%
- **Organic/GMB**: 25%
- **Repeat Customers**: 20%
- **Referrals**: 10%

## Service Mix
- **Plumbing**: 40%
- **HVAC**: 35%
- **Electrical**: 25%

## Opportunities Identified
1. Facebook/Nextdoor advertising for community presence
