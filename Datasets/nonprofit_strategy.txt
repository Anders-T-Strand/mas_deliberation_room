# Current Marketing Strategy - Nonprofit Organization

## Organization Overview
- **Mission**: Environmental Conservation & Education
- **Annual Budget**: $2.4M
- **Donor Base**: 4,200 active donors
- **Programs**: Wildlife protection, youth education, habitat restoration
- **Geographic Reach**: Regional (3-state area)

## Current Marketing Mix

### 1. Email Marketing (35% of budget)
- **List Size**: 18,000 contacts
- **Send Frequency**: 2x monthly newsletter, targeted appeals
- **Open Rate**: 25%
- **Click Rate**: 12%
- **Email-to-Donation Rate**: 16%

### 2. Social Media & Content (25% of budget)
- **Facebook**: 15K followers, community-focused content
- **Instagram**: 8K followers, impact stories and wildlife photos
- **LinkedIn**: 3K followers, corporate partnership focus
- **Blog**: Weekly impact stories
- **Video Content**: Monthly donor spotlights

### 3. Direct Mail (20% of budget)
- **Annual Appeal**: November, 4,000 pieces
- **Spring Campaign**: April, 3,500 pieces
- **Major Donor Communications**: Quarterly impact reports
- **Response Rate**: 5.2%

### 4. Events & Community Outreach (15% of budget)
- **Annual Gala**: $150K raised, 300 attendees
- **Community Events**: 10-12 per year
- **Corporate Volunteering**: Monthly opportunities
- **School Programs**: Reaching 5,000 students annually

### 5. Digital Advertising (5% of budget)
- **Google Ad Grants**: $10K monthly in-kind
- **Facebook/Instagram**: Awareness and event promotion
- **LinkedIn**: Corporate sponsorship campaigns

## Current Challenges
1. **Donor Retention**: 72% (below 75% benchmark)
2. **Aging Donor Base**: Average age 58
3. **Recurring Giving**: Only 39% on monthly programs
4. **Corporate Funding**: Decreased 15% YoY
5. **Grant Competition**: Success rate down to 25%

## Key Metrics
- **Average Donation**: $135
- **Monthly Donors**: 1,650 (39% of base)
- **Major Gifts (>$1,000)**: 4-5 per month
- **Corporate Sponsors**: 15 active
- **Grant Funding**: $40K-50K monthly
- **Volunteer Hours**: 450-550 monthly

## Revenue Sources
- **Individual Donations**: 55%
- **Grants**: 25%
- **Corporate Sponsorships**: 10%
- **Events**: 8%
- **Planned Giving**: 2%

## Donor Segments
- **Major Donors ($1,000+)**: 5%
- **Mid-Level ($250-999)**: 15%
- **General Donors (<$250)**: 65%
- **Monthly Sustainers**: 15%
