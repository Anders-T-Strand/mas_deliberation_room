# Current Marketing Strategy - B2B SaaS

## Business Overview
- **Industry**: B2B SaaS (Enterprise Software)
- **Target Audience**: Mid-market to Enterprise (500-5000 employees)
- **Average Deal Size**: $15,000
- **Sales Cycle**: 40-45 days
- **Current MRR**: $1.6M
- **Annual Churn Rate**: ~20%

## Current Marketing Mix

### 1. Content Marketing & SEO (35% of budget)
- **Blog Posts**: 8-10 per month
- **Whitepapers/Ebooks**: 2 per quarter
- **Webinars**: 3 per month
- **Organic Traffic**: 45,000 monthly visitors
- **MQL Conversion**: 3.2%

### 2. Paid Search & LinkedIn Ads (40% of budget)
- **Google Ads Focus**: High-intent keywords
- **LinkedIn Campaign Types**: Sponsored content, InMail
- **Average CPL**: $226
- **MQL to SQL Rate**: 29%

### 3. Sales Enablement (15% of budget)
- **Sales Collateral**: Case studies, battle cards, demos
- **Sales Team Size**: 15 reps
- **SDR Team**: 8 reps
- **Average Calls per Rep**: 50/day

### 4. Events & Partnerships (10% of budget)
- **Trade Shows**: 4 per year
- **Virtual Events**: Monthly
- **Partner Program**: 25 active partners
- **Partner-sourced Leads**: 15% of pipeline

## Current Challenges
1. **Long Sales Cycles**: 40-45 days average
2. **High CAC**: $8,500 per customer
3. **SQL to Opportunity Conversion**: Only 67%
4. **Marketing-Sales Alignment**: Lead quality concerns

## Key Metrics
- **Monthly Marketing Spend**: ~$37,000
- **Monthly Sales Spend**: ~$52,000
- **MQLs per Month**: 160-170
- **SQLs per Month**: 48-52
- **Closed Won per Month**: 10-11

## Lead Sources
- **Inbound Marketing**: 40%
- **Outbound Sales**: 35%
- **Partners**: 15%
- **Events**: 10%

## Opportunities Identified
1. Account-Based Marketing (ABM) implementation
2. Customer advocacy program
3. Podcast sponsorships in relevant industries
4. Interactive product demos
5. Expansion into new verticals (Healthcare, Financial Services)
6. Marketing automation optimization
