# Current Marketing Strategy - Wellness Coaching

## Business Overview
- **Industry**: Health & Wellness Coaching
- **Services**: 1-on-1 coaching, group programs, workshops
- **Target Audience**: Professional women 35-55, health-conscious individuals
- **Average Program Value**: $475
- **Active Client Base**: 200-220
- **Program Duration**: 3-6 months

## Current Marketing Mix

### 1. Content Marketing & Social Media (40% of budget)
- **Instagram**: Daily posts, Reels 3x/week, 12K followers
- **Blog**: 2 posts/week on wellness topics
- **YouTube**: Weekly videos, 3.5K subscribers
- **Podcast**: Bi-weekly episodes, 1,500 downloads/episode
- **Email List**: 8,500 subscribers

### 2. Referral Program (25% of budget)
- **Client Referrals**: 25% of new business
- **Referral Incentive**: $100 credit per successful referral
- **Professional Network**: Partnerships with 5 local gyms/studios
- **Healthcare Providers**: Relationships with 3 functional medicine doctors

### 3. Paid Advertising (20% of budget)
- **Facebook/Instagram Ads**: Lead magnets, webinar signups
- **Google Ads**: Limited to high-intent keywords
- **Average CPL**: $35
- **Webinar to Client Conversion**: 15%

### 4. Speaking & Workshops (15% of budget)
- **Monthly Workshops**: Average 30 attendees
- **Corporate Wellness Talks**: 2-3 per quarter
- **Virtual Summit Participation**: 4-5 per year
- **Conversion from Events**: 20%

## Current Challenges
1. **Inconsistent Monthly Revenue**: $8K-$17K variation
2. **Program Completion Rate**: 67% (want 80%+)
3. **Time-Intensive Model**: Limited to 40 clients max
4. **Seasonal Demand**: January spike, summer lull
5. **Price Sensitivity**: Losing prospects to cheaper online programs

## Key Metrics
- **Monthly New Clients**: 18-20
- **Client Retention Rate**: 84%
- **Average Sessions per Client**: 4.3
- **Client Satisfaction Score**: 4.8/5.0
- **Monthly Revenue**: $50,000-65,000

## Lead Sources
- **Referrals**: 25%
- **Social Media**: 30%
- **Organic Search**: 20%
- **Paid Ads**: 15%
- **Email Marketing**: 10%

## Opportunities Identified
1. Group coaching programs to improve scalability
2. Digital course creation for passive income
3. Corporate wellness packages
4. Mobile app for client accountability
5. Affiliate partnerships with wellness brands
6. TikTok presence for younger demographic
7. Membership/subscription model
8. Automated email nurture sequences
