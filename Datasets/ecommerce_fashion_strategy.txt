# Current Marketing Strategy - Fashion Ecommerce

## Business Overview
- **Industry**: Fashion Ecommerce
- **Target Audience**: Women 25-45, urban professionals, fashion-conscious
- **Average Order Value**: $162.50
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: $488.00
- **Current Monthly Revenue**: ~$1.5M

## Current Marketing Mix

### 1. Instagram Marketing (60% of budget)
- **Daily Spend**: $500-650
- **Content Strategy**: 
  - 15-25 posts per day
  - Mix of product shots, lifestyle content, user-generated content
  - Stories featuring behind-the-scenes and flash sales
- **Engagement Rate**: 4.2% average
- **Follower Growth**: ~2,000/month
- **Conversion Rate**: 3.0-3.3%

### 2. Google Ads (25% of budget)
- **Focus**: Shopping campaigns and branded search
- **Average CPC**: $2.15
- **Conversion Rate**: 2.8%

### 3. Email Marketing (10% of budget)
- **List Size**: 45,000 subscribers
- **Send Frequency**: 3x per week
- **Open Rate**: 22%
- **Click Rate**: 3.5%

### 4. Influencer Partnerships (5% of budget)
- **Micro-influencers**: 10-15 per month
- **Engagement focus**: Unboxing videos, outfit posts

## Current Challenges
1. **High CAC**: $65-70 per customer vs $488 LTV
2. **Seasonal Revenue Fluctuations**: 30% variance month-to-month
3. **Low Repeat Purchase Rate**: Only 40% of customers make second purchase
4. **Instagram Algorithm Changes**: Declining organic reach

## Key Metrics
- **Monthly Marketing Spend**: ~$580,000
- **Overall ROAS**: 2.6x
- **New vs Returning Customer Split**: 60/40
- **Mobile Traffic**: 75%

## Opportunities Identified
1. TikTok expansion (currently not utilized)
2. SMS marketing implementation
3. Loyalty program enhancement
4. Pinterest advertising for discovery
5. Personalization engine for email/site
