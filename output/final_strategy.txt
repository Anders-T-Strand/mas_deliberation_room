## Fashion Ecommerce: Comprehensive Marketing Plan

**Executive Summary:**

This comprehensive marketing plan addresses identified weaknesses within the current fashion ecommerce strategy, focusing on high Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), low repeat purchase rates, underutilization of emerging platforms, seasonal revenue fluctuations, and low email engagement. The plan integrates TikTok and SMS marketing, enhances the loyalty program, personalizes email campaigns, diversifies product offerings, and aims to drive significant revenue growth while improving key performance indicators (KPIs).

**1. Situation Analysis:**

*   **Industry:** Fashion Ecommerce
*   **Target Audience:** Women 25-45, urban professionals, fashion-conscious.
*   **Average Order Value (AOV):** \$162.50
*   **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV):** \$488.00
*   **Current Monthly Revenue:** ~$1.5M
*   **Key Weaknesses:**
    *   High CAC (\$65-\$70)
    *   Low Repeat Purchase Rate (40%)
    *   Underutilization of TikTok and SMS
    *   Seasonal Revenue Fluctuations (30% variance)
    *   Low Email Engagement (22% open rate)

**2. Objectives:**

*   Reduce CAC to align with industry benchmarks (aiming for 25% of CLTV or lower).
*   Increase repeat purchase rate from 40% to 60% within 12 months.
*   Increase revenue through emerging platforms (TikTok and SMS)
*   Decrease seasonal revenue fluctuations
*   Improve email open rates and click-through rates.
*   Grow Overall Revenue by 25%

**3. Strategies and Tactics:**

**3.1 TikTok and SMS Marketing Implementation:**

*   **Goal:** Increase brand awareness, engagement, and drive sales through TikTok and SMS channels.
*   **Tactics:**
    *   **TikTok:**
        *   Launch a TikTok campaign targeting the core demographic.
        *   Collaborate with fashion influencers and micro-influencers to create engaging content.
        *   Run paid TikTok advertising campaigns focused on product promotion and brand awareness.
        *   Encourage user-generated content (UGC) campaigns with branded hashtags.
    *   **SMS:**
        *   Implement an SMS marketing platform for promotional offers, shipping updates, and cart abandonment reminders.
        *   Build an SMS subscriber list through website opt-ins and contests.
        *   Send targeted SMS messages based on customer segmentation and purchase history.
*   **Budget:** \$75,000 (TikTok: \$50,000 (influencer collaboration and paid ads), SMS: \$25,000(platform subscription, and initial campaign))
*   **Timeline:**
    *   **Phase 1 (Months 1-3):** TikTok Campaign launch (Influencer Outreach, Content Creation, Paid Ad Testing), SMS platform implementation, SMS subscriber acquisition.
